The sinister overlords of the //Friday Python Circle// are alarmed by the popularity of the //Saturday Java Club//. They plan to infiltrate the Club and seed discord and doubt. The Circle's tumultuous plan is good but not perfect. Circle agitators can sometimes be recognized by the way they communicate: it reeks of Python. Lots of empty spaces and overuse of underscores, are some of the signs. The Saturday Java Club becomes aware of the Circle's malevolent plans. In response, the Club posts a guard outside the place it meets. The guard's job is to tell if somone arriving to the meeting is a Circle inflitrator or a pure-at-heart, loyal member of the Club. You. Are. The. Guard.
